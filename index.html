Our Story...

Discover how we transformed from a small start-up to a global leader

Our journey began as a small, passionate start-up, driven by a love for trading cards. From day one, we focused on curating a unique collection and delivering exceptional customer service. Our goal was simple: to offer a personal touch and become the go-to place for card enthusiasts.

As we grew, the company evolved into a beloved small business. Our carefully chosen range of trading cards expanded, and we honed our online platform to enhance the shopping experience. We started to build a community, focusing on reliable service and creating connections with our growing customer base.

With our reputation solidified, we became a local favorite. We expanded our reach within the community, offering local pickup and hosting events for fellow collectors. Our dedication to quality and personalized service turned us into a trusted local hub for all things trading cards.

Our ambitions grew more, and the company took its first steps onto the continental stage. We embraced international shipping and diversified our inventory to cater to a wider audience. Our platform adapted to multiple languages and currencies, reflecting our commitment to reaching card lovers across borders.

Today, CardShop is a global player in the trading card market. Our expansive online store serves enthusiasts around the world with a vast selection and efficient global shipping. We take pride in connecting collectors from different continents, delivering exceptional service, and offering rare finds no matter where you are.
